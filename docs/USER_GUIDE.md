# User Guide for Ramayana Sustainability Training

## Getting Started
To begin your journey with the Ramayana Sustainability Training program, follow these steps:
1. **Create an Account**: Visit the registration page and fill in your details to create an account.
2. **Login**: After registration, login using your credentials.
3. **Explore the Dashboard**: Familiarize yourself with the user dashboard where you can access training modules and track your progress.

## Learning Modules
The training program is divided into several modules covering various aspects of sustainability. Each module includes:
- **Interactive Content**: Engaging learning materials including videos, articles, and quizzes.
- **Assessments**: Evaluate your understanding of the material through formative and summative assessments.

## Chatbot Usage
The integrated chatbot, named **Sustainify**, is available to assist you:
- **Ask Questions**: You can ask Sustainify about module content, scheduling, and more.
- **Receive Feedback**: Use the chatbot to get tips on improving your training routine.

## Progress Tracking
You can track your progress through:
- **Statistics Dashboard**: View your completed modules, quiz scores, and time spent on each module.
- **Personalized Reports**: Receive suggestions based on your learning style and performance.

## Leaderboard
Engage with the community by checking the leaderboard:
- **Top Performers**: See how you rank against other learners.
- **Incentives**: Compete for digital badges and certificates by achieving high scores in modules.

## Profile Management
Manage your profile easily:
- **Edit Profile**: Update your personal information, profile picture, and preferences.
- **Privacy Settings**: Control who can see your progress and leaderboard rankings.

## FAQs
Find answers to common queries:
- **How do I reset my password?**  Follow the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
- **What to do if I encounter technical issues?** Contact support through the Help section.
